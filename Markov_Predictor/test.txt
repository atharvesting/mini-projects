The cat sat on the mat in the house all day long. Every morning the cat would wake up and stretch its paws. Then it would jump down from the bed and walk to the kitchen. In the kitchen, the cat found its bowl of food waiting on the floor. The cat ate the food quickly and then drank some water from the other bowl. After breakfast, the cat went back to the living room and sat on the mat again. The mat was soft and warm, just the way the cat liked it.

Outside the house, the sun was shining bright in the sky. Birds were flying from tree to tree singing happy songs. The dog in the neighbor's yard barked at the birds but they didn't care. They kept flying and singing all morning long. Inside, the cat watched the birds through the window. The cat's tail moved back and forth as it stared at the window. Sometimes the cat would meow softly wanting to go outside.

In the afternoon, the family came home from work and school. The little girl ran to the cat and picked it up for a hug. The cat purred loudly and rubbed its head against her hand. The girl sat on the couch with the cat on her lap. They watched TV together while eating snacks. The cat licked some crumbs from the girl's fingers. Later, the boy came in with his ball and started bouncing it on the floor. The cat watched the ball move up and down, up and down. It tried to catch the ball with its paw but missed every time.

At night, the house became quiet and dark. The family went to bed in their rooms upstairs. The cat stayed downstairs on the mat near the warm heater. It curled up into a ball and closed its eyes. The cat dreamed of chasing birds and playing with yarn all night long. In the morning, the cat would wake up again and start the day just like before. The cat sat on the mat, ate its food, watched the birds, and waited for the family to come home. Day after day, the cat lived a happy life in the house on the soft mat.
